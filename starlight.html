Starlight Theatre

What is Starlight Theatre?
Starlight Theatre is Rock Valley Colleges Theatre building and location where many Preforming Arts majors study and do shows over the summers. Starlight has many productions through the summer and Starlight Theatre is always looking for people to help volunteer and always looking for other actors.
Auditioning and Volunteers!
Starlight Theatre always accepts anyone who wishes to become an actor into the stage. From large well known musicals/plays to smaller productions, Starlight is open to all! If acting is not your thing but still wanna be a part of Starlight, no problem! We offer volunteer positions and we are always looking for people to help during show nights.
Costume Rentals
Starlight Theatre is proud to support other local schools and theatres with costumes for other productions! Starlight Theatre has over 380,000 costumes and each is available for rental. You can contact us at 815-921-2160. 815-921-2160
