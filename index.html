Welcome to Rock Valley College

Rock Valley College (RVC) is located in Rockford, IL where students from local high schools and people wishing to gain degrees in specific fields have the oppourtunity to come and learn in this ever-evolving campus, adding more and more options for degrees over many years.

A day in Rock Vally College

A simple day at Rock Valley College consists of many things ranging from classes, moving to and from classes, getting food, studying, and working with classmates. Many of our RVC students take time in between classes to meet with peers and work along with other classmates. Since RVC is a local college many go out to eat in the town or others enjoy time in the student center. Fit with break rooms, a computer lab, and food stations to serve students.

Activities at RVC

Students at Rock Valley College have options to spend time with classmatmes or to participate in the local clubs that the college has. Students spend time in these activities persuing what they love, and along the way, making new and long-lasting friends. Sports is also a part of the college, involving those that come from many different backgrounds to try new sports that seem unfamiliar to them. Some activities in the campus may include:

  • Preforming arts/theater located in Starlight Theater on campus.
  • Clubs suchs as the Gamers Club, Anime Club, Chess Club, and many more.
  • Sports such as baseball, softball, basketball, football, and soccer.
  • The local Newspaper is a large part of Rock Valley College and being able to communicate to the people on campus.
