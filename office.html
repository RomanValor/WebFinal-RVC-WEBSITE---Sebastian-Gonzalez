What is the Admissions Office?

Rock Valley Colleges Admissions Office is where students who wish to come and study at Rock Valley College come and apply and begin the process of being allowed into RVC. Rock Valley College is open to everyone from students in high school to people who have had 20+ years of work experience. Rock Valley is here for everyone to come and learn new things in this ever-growing campus!

Options for High School Students

Rock Valley College offeres many different programs that can help young students who wish to persue college early achieve these goals and offer guidance for their educational future. Rock Valley College gives choices such as Duel Credit at RVC where students in high school can gain credit towards their high school diploma and their college degree.

Things Parents Should Know

As a parent for an aspiring college student, there are many things you should know when it comes to your childs future at Rock Valley College, and we are here to let you know some information that can help you!

  • Enrolling to Rock Valley College

    • Your child can enroll to RVC at any time! Rock Valley College offers guidance and is happy to provide any more information for you to help you on your decision if Rock Valley College is right for your child.

  • Placement Testing and What it means

    • Placement Testing is a test that Rock Valley College provides for students that have decided to come and study at RVC. Every new student in Rock Valley College must complete this placement test so The school can assess your childs knowledge and see where they should be placed to start school at RVC.

  • Financial Aid

    • Students who come into Rock Valley College have the option to complete a Free Application for Federal Student Aid and determine if that student is eligible for financial aid. Financial Aid is open to all students, but if you have more questions, you can always visit our financial aid page.
